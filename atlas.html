Atlas

Click here to visit the Atlas app

The Rice Atlas app is a user-freindly way to locate any building on Rice's campus. Simply type the name of the building you wish to find into the app's search bar, and you will be shown it's exact location via Google Maps. Looking for more information about a specific place on campus? The search function also provides a link that you can follow to find more info about your destination. In addition, the Atlas app allows you to see the current location of all of the Rice shuttles on and around campus. This app is perfect for visitors to Rice University, students trying to locate their classes, and more!
